Program Overview

ArvGuard LLC ("ArvGuard," "we," "our," or "us") operates an SMS messaging program to support users of the ArvGuard Digital Executor platform. These messages are an integral part of our alive check system, vault security monitoring, and executor coordination services.

This policy applies to all SMS communications sent by ArvGuard to users who have opted in to receive text message notifications. By opting in, you agree to the terms of this policy, our Terms of Service, and our Privacy Policy.

Program Name

ArvGuard Digital Executor SMS Notifications ‚Äî Recurring automated messages related to vault activity, alive check reminders, and executor status updates. This is not a marketing program.

02

How We Obtain Consent

ArvGuard only sends SMS messages to users who have provided express written consent. Consent is collected through our SMS opt-in page at arvguard.com/sms-consent, where users must:

  • Provide a valid U.S. mobile phone number
  • Check an explicit opt-in checkbox with clear consent language
  • Acknowledge that message and data rates may apply
  • Acknowledge they can opt out at any time by replying STOP
  • Review links to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

Consent to receive SMS messages is never a condition of purchasing or using ArvGuard services. Users may use the full ArvGuard platform without enabling SMS notifications.

Consent records, including timestamp, IP address, phone number, and opt-in page URL, are retained for a minimum of four (4) years in accordance with TCPA recordkeeping requirements.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important ‚Äî Alive Check Impact

If you opt out of SMS and your alive check schedule relies on SMS-based check-ins, your account will fall back to email-only check-ins. We strongly recommend updating your alive check settings in the app after opting out to ensure your executor process is not accidentally triggered. Opting out of SMS does not deactivate your ArvGuard account.

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message: "You have been unsubscribed from ArvGuard SMS alerts. No further messages will be sent. Reply START to re-subscribe."

To re-subscribe after opting out, reply START to any previous ArvGuard message, or visit arvguard.com/sms-consent.

Data & Privacy

Your mobile phone number and SMS consent records are handled in accordance with ArvGuard's Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • Your phone number is used solely for ArvGuard service communications
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third-party marketers
  • Your number may be shared with our SMS delivery provider (e.g., Twilio) solely for the purpose of transmitting messages
  • Opt-in records are retained for a minimum of 4 years for TCPA compliance
  • You may request deletion of your phone number by contacting support (subject to legal retention requirements)

ArvGuard complies with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), CTIA guidelines, and all applicable state regulations governing SMS marketing and transactional messaging.

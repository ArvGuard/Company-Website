Common questions

How do I opt out of SMS notifications?
Reply STOP to any ArvGuard text message. You'll receive one final confirmation and no further messages will be sent. You can also manage notification preferences in your account settings.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from your account Settings → Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. Email support@arvguard.com if you need help.
What happens to my vault if I cancel?
Your vault data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, giving you time to export your information. After 30 days, your encrypted vault is permanently deleted. We cannot recover it after deletion.
I didn't get my alive check reminder. What happened?
Check that your phone number is correct in account settings and that you haven't opted out of SMS. If the issue continues, email support@arvguard.com with your account email address.
Can I request deletion of my data?
Yes. Email legal@arvguard.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and your account email. We'll process the request within 30 days in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
I found a security vulnerability. How do I report it?
Email security@arvguard.com with details. Please do not disclose publicly until we've had a chance to address the issue. We take all security reports seriously and will respond promptly.

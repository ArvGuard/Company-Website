How It Works

Six steps.
Set up once.
Protected forever.

1

Create your digital map

Open the app and start adding accounts — banking, crypto, email, utilities, subscriptions, business tools. Most people don't realize how many accounts they have until they map it out. You're building an inventory, not giving passwords.

2

Add instructions for each asset

For each account, write what should happen: "close this," "transfer to my spouse," "download photos and send to my sister," "contact this advisor." This is what your family actually needs — not just access, but guidance.

3

Secure the vault

Add sensitive items — recovery codes, crypto seed phrases, insurance policies, important documents. Everything is encrypted on your device using AES-256. ArvGuard cannot read it. Nobody can without your passphrase.

4

Add trusted contacts

Choose 2–3 trusted people — a spouse, sibling, business partner. They verify their identity and become part of your recovery quorum. They don't get access. They just become the key holders — and no one key unlocks everything alone.

5

The alive check

Every 30 days, we send one question: "Are you OK?" You tap yes in seconds. If you stop responding, contacts are notified — but you're warned first and can cancel instantly with one tap. Nothing happens without your awareness.

6

Safe, staged release

If something happens, your trusted contacts initiate the release process. Stage 1 releases your account inventory and instructions. Stage 2 releases full vault access — only after quorum verification and death proof. Your family gets exactly what they need, nothing more.

03
Security Architecture

Your vault is
yours alone.

Not even ArvGuard can read your data. Every item in your vault is encrypted on your device before it ever touches our servers. We operate on a zero-knowledge architecture — meaning even if we were breached, your data remains protected.

A quorum of trusted contacts is required to reconstruct access. No single person — not your spouse, not your attorney, not us — can open your vault alone.

🔐AES-256-GCM Encryption
🔑Argon2id Key Derivation
🧩Shamir's Secret Sharing
☁️Zero Plaintext Storage
🔒

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

All encryption and decryption happens on your device. Our servers only ever see ciphertext — your plaintext never leaves your phone.

🧩

Threshold Recovery

Your vault master key is split into shards using Shamir's Secret Sharing. A minimum quorum (e.g., 2 of 3 contacts) must cooperate to reconstruct it.

📋

Staged Release Protocol

Account inventory releases first. Full vault access requires additional verification — preventing premature or unauthorized releases.

🛡️

Immutable Audit Log

Every action — login, edit, release attempt — is logged. Any unauthorized attempt alerts all trusted contacts immediately.

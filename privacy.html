Who We Are

ArvGuard LLC ("ArvGuard," "we," "our," or "us") is a digital executor platform headquartered in Star, Idaho. We provide a secure vault service that allows individuals to store critical digital account information and designate trusted executors who can access that information upon verified incapacitation or death.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of arvguard.com, arvguard.app, and any related mobile applications or services (collectively, the "Service").

Questions? Contact us at privacy@arvguard.com.

02

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the Service safely and effectively. We categorize data into two types: information we can read, and information we mathematically cannot read.

Information We Can Access
  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number (if SMS enabled), account creation date
  • Executor contacts: Names and email addresses of your designated executors (not encrypted, as we must contact them)
  • Alive check configuration: Your check-in schedule, escalation preferences, and current alive check state
  • Usage data: Login timestamps, IP addresses, device type, browser/OS for security and fraud prevention
  • Communications: Support emails, feedback, and other messages you send us
  • Payment data: Billing information processed through our payment provider (Stripe). We do not store full card numbers.
  • SMS consent records: Phone number, opt-in timestamp, IP address, and opt-in page URL (retained for TCPA compliance)
üîí Information We Cannot Access (Zero-Knowledge)
  • Vault contents: All documents, passwords, account credentials, and files stored in your vault are encrypted on your device before transmission. We receive only ciphertext ‚Äî the decryption key never leaves your device.
  • Vault item metadata: Names, labels, and categories you assign to vault items are also encrypted client-side.
  • Secret shares: Your Shamir's Secret Sharing key fragments are never combined or accessible on our servers.

This is not a policy choice ‚Äî it is a mathematical guarantee. AES-256-GCM encryption with client-side key derivation (Argon2id) means your vault content is inaccessible to us by design.

We do not collect Social Security numbers, government ID numbers, biometric data, or health information.

03

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

ArvGuard is built on a zero-knowledge architecture. This means your vault is encrypted on your device using a key derived from your master password before any data is transmitted to our servers. We store only encrypted ciphertext ‚Äî the plaintext content of your vault is never accessible to ArvGuard employees, systems, or legal process.

How it works
  • Your master password is processed through Argon2id to derive a Vault Master Key (VMK)
  • Individual vault items are encrypted with unique Data Encryption Keys (DEKs) using AES-256-GCM
  • DEKs are wrapped (encrypted) with your VMK before storage
  • Your VMK never leaves your device in plaintext form
  • Executor access uses Shamir's Secret Sharing (2-of-3) ‚Äî no single party holds a complete key

Consequence: If you forget your master password, we cannot recover your vault contents. There is no "forgot password" that unlocks your encrypted data. We can only reset your account (deleting your vault) or help you initiate your recovery process if you have set one up.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Authenticating your account, operating alive check schedules, sending executor notifications
  • Security: Detecting fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse; maintaining audit logs
  • Communications: Sending transactional emails and SMS messages (if opted in) related to your account
  • Support: Responding to your questions and troubleshooting issues
  • Legal compliance: Meeting our obligations under applicable law including TCPA, CCPA, and data protection regulations
  • Service improvement: Analyzing aggregate, anonymized usage patterns to improve the platform

We do not use your information for advertising, sell it to data brokers, or use it to train AI models.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted vendors who help us operate the Service, including AWS (cloud infrastructure), Stripe (payments), Twilio (SMS delivery), and similar. These providers are contractually bound to use your data only to provide services to us.
  • Executor release: When a verified release process completes, we facilitate the delivery of encrypted vault contents to your designated executor(s). They receive the same ciphertext we store ‚Äî decryption requires the Shamir key reconstruction process.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by valid legal process (court order, subpoena). Because we cannot decrypt vault contents, any such disclosure would include only the unencrypted account metadata described in Section 2.
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you and honor existing privacy commitments.
  • Safety: We may disclose information to protect the safety of any person or to prevent fraud or illegal activity.
06

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active and for 2 years after deletion, then purged
  • Encrypted vault contents: Retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Audit logs: Retained for 7 years (required for executor process integrity)
  • SMS consent records: Retained for 4 years (TCPA compliance requirement)
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years (tax and financial compliance)
  • Support communications: Retained for 3 years after resolution

You may request deletion of your account at any time. Upon deletion, we will purge your personal data on the schedule above, except where retention is required by law.

07

Security

ArvGuard uses industry-standard and beyond-standard security practices:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for all vault contents (client-side)
  • Argon2id key derivation (resistant to GPU and ASIC brute-force attacks)
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication (TOTP) required for sensitive operations
  • Immutable audit logging with 50+ event types
  • AWS infrastructure with SOC 2-compliant controls
  • Regular penetration testing and security reviews

No system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique master password and enable MFA on your account. In the event of a security breach affecting your unencrypted account data, we will notify you as required by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of SMS: Reply STOP to any message or visit arvguard.com/sms-consent
  • Opt-out of marketing: Unsubscribe from any promotional emails via the link in those emails
  • California residents (CCPA): Right to know, delete, and opt out of sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@arvguard.com. We will respond within 30 days.
